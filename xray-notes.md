# X-Ray Basics

## Definition
X-rays are a form of electromagnetic radiation used for diagnostic imaging.

## Key Points
- Short wavelength, high energy
- Can penetrate soft tissues
- Absorbed by dense structures like bone

## Uses
- Fracture detection
- Chest imaging
- Dental imaging

## Advantages
- Fast
- Cost-effective

## Limitations
- Radiation exposure
- Limited soft tissue detail
